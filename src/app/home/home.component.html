About Us

Sr. Cuidado is a company founded on the idea of changing the setting for those most in need of care; creating appointments easily accessible, at home, which is where the elderly feel most comfortable, and above all, taking care of all fundamental areas, in addition to physical health, we have technically qualified professionals to handle any situation. For mental health, providing caregivers with care humanized and ensure that all our patients have what they need, respect, affection and care.

Mission

Promote, above all, the quality of life and well-being of patients, bringing comfort, welcome and help to families. Deliver the best and most qualified workforce in terms of caregivers. Always offer the humanized, personalized service, with respect and affection that we promise, thus guaranteeing the safety and tranquility of our customers.

View

To become a reference name in quality and excellence in the area of elderly care. Provide training and opportunity for our caregivers, considering that to perform this function it is necessary to have extensive knowledge.

Values

Ethics in work and health, commitment and responsibility to fulfill what we promise, commitment to care, transparency and security in the relationship with our customers and collaborators, respect and appreciation of people.

Im Elderly

Reaching the Third Age is a gift, but also a delicate part of life, where those who cared for the most now need care. That is why, at Sr.Cuidado, we want to reciprocate, providing qualified caregivers, a humanized and personalized service for you, with professionals able to deal with different situations, who help, not only in health but in the social well-being and mentality of their patients.

Our Services:

Elderly Caregivers.

We believe in the importance of HomeCare service, promoting convenience and safety, therefore, we assist from the basic care to the most specialized, according to our customers.

Home medication application.

Working responsibly to avoid self-medication, wrong application and even errors in dosages, we provide experienced caregivers in applications and medications, ensuring the complete process security.

Caregivers of elderly people with physical limitations.

We know that reduced mobility makes it difficult to carry out activities everyday activities and even simple movements, therefore, we provide experienced professionals in specific techniques for help in situations where our patients need planning motor.

Caregivers of elderly people with disabilities.

Disabilities have many causes and many associated limitations. in each case, for each of these cases, Sr.Cuidado provides a experienced and trained caregiver to meet every need and, more importantly, assist in the assisted autonomy of our patients.

Im Caregiver

We know that the life expectancy of Brazilians has grown, and people are living longer and with that, there is a need for Professional Caregivers, with different vital functions, which vary according to their patients, playing a fundamental role in supporting these people's lives.

Therefore, we at Sr. Cuidado want you, a professionally trained and qualified, who wants to follow his vocation and add value, come to be part of this team and take care of those who have always taken care of someone.
